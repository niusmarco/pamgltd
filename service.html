Our Services

Family Office

A unique boutique family office for high-net-worth families, PAMG Family Office provides comprehensive wealth and risk management, as well as a full range of family services, enabling the long-term development of assets in line with the family's expectations, and facilitating the smooth transfer and value enhancement of assets across generations.  We work with top tier global private banks to cater to your needs.

Family Trust Setup

PAMG Family Office is highly experienced in establishing new family trusts or working seamlessly with your existing structures. Our firm excels in resolving challenges related to outdated trust setups, facilitating the efficient transfer of assets in and out of trusts, and restructuring trusts for effective multi-generational estate planning. We specialize in supporting high-net-worth families to ensure their trusts remain robust, flexible, and aligned with long-term legacy and succession goals.

Tax Planning

Drawing on Hong Kong's leading financial industry, we partner with families to create personalized wealth strategies that deliever competitive returns and complete peace of mind. Our expert advisors combine sophisticated asset allocation with Common Reporting System-compliant reporting, ensuring your finances are both optimized and fully transparent for the future.

Real Estate Investment

We are managed by experienced real estate professionals. Through integrated financial operations, trust planning, diversified asset portfolios, and customized tax strategies, we provide our valued clients with comprehensive services, including commercial and residential property sales, rental management arrangements, and other tailored solutions. We act as personal stewards of our clients’ real estate investments. Over the years, our reputation has been established among clients from China, Hong Kong, and the immigrant/entrant community for our commitment to excellence and reliability.
