About Us

Company Profile

‍

‍
‍PAMG Ltd. is a Hong Kong-based financial group serving high-net-worth clients and families internationally.

We are a licensed institution under the Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission (SFC). Our integrated service model combines dedicated family office services from our Hong Kong headquarters with global asset management delivered through our offices in both Hong Kong and Canada. This structure allows us to provide holistic wealth and legacy planning for families in Asia, supported by professional asset management and direct market access in both Asian and North American markets.

Client asset safety is our priority. We build long-term partnerships based on independent analysis, integrity, and tailored strategies—whether managing a family’s complete financial ecosystem or growing a specific investment portfolio.

Hong Kong has always been a core hub of global finance. Its deep talent pool and robust legal system have established its indispensable position. By leveraging these strengths and our strategic presence in Canada, we are positioned as a reliable partner for clients who value clarity, security, and thoughtful growth across generations.
